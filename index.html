What are BPS?

One basis point (bps) = 0.01% = 0.0001 (decimal)

  • Used to express small changes in interest rates, fees, yields.
  • 100 bps = 1%

Why use basis points?

  • Removes ambiguity when discussing small percentage changes.
  • Useful in finance: loans, bonds, margins, expense ratios.
